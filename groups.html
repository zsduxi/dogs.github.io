There are many breeds of dogs and they are classified in groups

  1. Terriers
  2. Sporting
  3. Hounds
  4. Non-Sporting
  5. Working
  6. Herding
  7. Toys

There are hundreds of different dog breeds. they come in a variety of shapes and sizes. for eg. a great dane can be up to 80 cm tall while a pomeranian can be up to 25 cm. each type of dogs have their own weight, look and shape.
